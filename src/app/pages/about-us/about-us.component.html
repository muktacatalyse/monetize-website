Who we are

Pocketsfull.ai is a cutting-edge monetization platform developed by Catalyse Research, a global leader in market research and data-driven insights. With over a decade of experience in conducting comprehensive market research studies across diverse industries and geographies, Catalyse Research brings unparalleled expertise to the digital monetization landscape.

At Pocketsfull.ai, we bridge the gap between market research and digital platforms by integrating high-quality, targeted surveys into web and mobile applications. Our platform empowers publishers and developers to unlock new revenue streams by seamlessly incorporating research opportunities that engage users and provide valuable insights to brands.

Backed by Catalyse Research's robust infrastructure and global reach, Pocketsfull.ai ensures that every survey is meticulously crafted and delivered to the right audience, enhancing user experience while generating meaningful data. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and ethical research practices positions us at the forefront of the digital monetization ecosystem.

Join us in transforming the way digital platforms engage with users and monetize content, all while contributing to impactful market research that drives business decisions worldwide.

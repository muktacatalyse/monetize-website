Users can access the offerwall within your platform. They can choose from a range of available offers, complete the required actions (e.g., completing a survey or downloading an app), and earn virtual currency or other rewards upon successful completion.

The offerwall provides various types of offers, including:

  • Surveys and questionnaires
  • App installations and interactions
  • Video views
  • Sign-ups for services or subscriptions
  • Product trials and purchases
  • Social media engagements

Users can earn rewards by completing the tasks or actions specified in the offers. After completing a task successfully, they will receive virtual currency, points, or other rewards as outlined in the offer or survey description.

Virtual currency is a form of digital credit earned through completing offers on the offerwall. This currency can be redeemed for a variety of rewards such as in-game items, gift cards, discounts, or even exchanged for real money, depending on the platform’s offerings.

Anyone using the platform or application integrated with the offerwall can participate. The offerwall is designed to engage active users who are interested in earning rewards by completing various offers and tasks.
